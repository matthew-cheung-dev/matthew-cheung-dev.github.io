About Me

The first game I remember playing was Frogger on my cousin's computer. However, my passion for games started while I was playing StarCraft Brood war on my parent's computer. After I found out I could make a living out of playing games competitively, I pursued as a professional gamer. As I got closer to the age that I could compete in professional competitions, my passion for games slowly transitioned into games development.

After finishing my studies in secondary school, I studied IT in Exeter College achieving a Level 3 BTEC Extended Diploma. I really enjoyed the coding aspect of my course and this helped in my decision to study Games Technology at University of the West of England(UWE). I am really happy that I chose UWE to further my studies into Games Technology
